About Us

The history of Ice Cream Kingdom Shop

Ice Cream Kingdom Company began in 1967, when John and Sally Smith decided to open their very own ice cream shop. Their vision was to provide the community with a bit of happiness by serving homemade ice cream. They held the philosophy that quality lies at the heart of every marvelous treat shop, and with this, John, master researcher, tinkerer and perfectionist, set out on a mission of making the best ice cream he could. After countless batches of ice cream, and tweaking and adjusting machinery, he came up with his own original, super-premium ice cream flavors to offer at their shop, located on Los Angeles, California.
